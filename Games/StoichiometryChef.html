🍲 Stoichiometry Chef

Balance the equation. Cook the dish. Don't blow up the kitchen.

Every recipe is an unbalanced chemical equation, like _H₂ + _O₂ → _H₂O. Use the − / + steppers on each ingredient to set the right whole-number coefficients — the same number of every atom must appear on both sides.

Hit Cook! when you think it's balanced. Get it right (in the simplest ratio) and you plate a dish. Get it wrong, and the cauldron... has opinions about that.

The cauldron's color mixes live from whatever you've poured in — watch it shift as you adjust ratios. Three blown burners and the kitchen closes.
